Healthy lifestyle and clean air are really important as they can prevent diseases and long time illness. Polluted air can lead to lung disease such as lung cancer. An Unhealthy lifestyle has many negative direct and Indirect results which can lead to various types of risks such as stress, sleep deprivation, obesity which can lead to heart problems further on. These problems need to be taken care of as they still continue on to increase in society.
